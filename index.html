Xiaopo Cheng

I obtained my PhD in Chemical and Biological Engineering from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in February 2025, where I worked with Prof. Michael D. Graham. My research centered on computational fluid dynamics, complex fluid flows, high-performance computing, and machine learning. I am particularly passionate about developing mathematical models, scalable parallel algorithms, and data-driven methods to gain deeper insights into physical, biological, and other complex phenomena in the real world. Feel free to contact me if you'd like to discuss or collaborate.

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science (2023) from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a Bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering (2019) from Tianjin University. I am currently a senior AI Developer Technology Engineer at NVIDIA.
