Xiaopo Cheng

I am a PhD candidate in Chemical and Biological Engineering at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, working with Prof. Michael D. Graham. My research centers on computational fluid dynamics, complex fluid flows, high-performance computing, and machine learning. I am particularly passionate about developing mathematical models, scalable parallel algorithms, and also data-driven methods to gain deeper insights into physical, biological, and other complex phenomena. Feel free to contact me if you'd like to discuss.

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science (2023) from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a Bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering (2019) from Tianjin University. I am expected to graduate with my PhD in February 2025, after which I will join NVIDIA as an AI Developer Technology Engineer.
